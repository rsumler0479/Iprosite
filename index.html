Who Are We?


Image Projections is a digital content development firm which creates, designs and transforms raw business content into turnkey solutions. Our partners and customers alike work together to deliver refreshing forms of digital media through mediums such as digital-out-of-home displays, POS systems , and web enabled devices. We believe in conveying an organizations key branding pillars through content curation and proper campaign deployments.

Benefits of our content solutions

Putting your audience first

Your audience is the greatest asset when it comes to building dynamic content. Determining what your viewers want and when they want is a task we dedicate night and day to at Image Projectors.

Content Management

Managing your content from remote locations enables your customers to always be updated on what new products and services you may be offering at special times like the holidays.

Consulting

In order to guarantee a truly useful and cost efficient project , we make sure we guide you along as your digital media campaign progresses.

In-House Commercials

We have a team full of videographers, script writers, and directors ready to deliver high quality in store commercials that emphasize your brands unique takeaways.

Updated News

Keeping your viewers informed with the latest news allows them to be up to date on the current times and seek your content for their daily insight into national and global perspectives.

Support

We provide you with excellent customer support , making sure that your digital media campaign is successfully deployed.
